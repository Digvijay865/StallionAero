STES Stallion Aero

Our team is divided into four specialized branches, each focusing on a critical aspect of UAV development and operations:


Avionics - Works on the electronic systems of UAVs, including flight controllers, sensors, communication systems, and automation for safe and reliable operations.


Design - Focuses on creating and optimizing UAV structures, aerodynamics, and innovative configurations to ensure performance and stability.


Analysis - Handles performance evaluation, simulations, computational studies (aerodynamics, structures, CFD, etc.), and data-driven improvements.


Non-Tech - Manages operations beyond engineering, including documentation, event coordination, outreach, sponsorships, and overall team management.


Together, these domains collaborate to balance technical innovation and operational efficiency, driving the team toward successful UAV development.
