✨ About STES Stallion Aero ✨
Founded in 2017, STES Stallion Aero is a passionate student club dedicated to the design, innovation, and research of UAVs (Unmanned Aerial Vehicles) and Fixed-Wing Aircraft. 🚀✈️
Over the years, our team has achieved remarkable milestones:
🏆 AIR 1 (2020) – for Video Representation at SAEINDIA
🏆 AIR 4 (2023) – at SAEINDIA DDC
We are driven by innovation, teamwork, and the vision to push the boundaries of aeronautical engineering and drone technology.
